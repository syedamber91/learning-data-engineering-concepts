My learning strategy is why-before-what: focus on ONE thing at a time, break it down to first principles, materialize it by writing code and deploying, and expose it for feedback through a blog or code review. In the AI era, DEs who stop understanding problems, making decisions, and evaluating trade-offs will be replaced — decision-making stays human while implementation is increasingly AI-assisted.

An LLM doesn't know facts; it's a probability distribution of language. It doesn't 'know' that Ha Noi is the capital of Vietnam — it learned from billions of sentences that when 'The capital of Vietnam is...' appears, 'Hanoi' has the highest chance of being correct. And LLMs aren't a 2020s breakthrough; they're a compounding process over many decades, with the 2017 Transformer paper as the key inflection point.

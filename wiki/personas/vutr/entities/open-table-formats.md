The modern table-format wave brought the table abstraction to the data lake: Apache Hive arrived from Meta in 2009, Databricks open-sourced Delta Lake in 2016, Uber ran Hudi on HDFS in production from 2016, and Netflix began developing Iceberg in 2017. As I like to remind people, this echoes an early effort more than 15 years ago to achieve the same thing these three formats are now chasing.

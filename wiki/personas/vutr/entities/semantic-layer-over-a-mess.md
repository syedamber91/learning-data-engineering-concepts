A semantic layer introduced hoping it will resolve an existing mess only ends up producing another mess — the same trap as a One Big Table thrown over unmodeled data. The mess lives in the missing modeling layer, not in the presentation surface on top of it; adding a layer above doesn't fix what's broken beneath.

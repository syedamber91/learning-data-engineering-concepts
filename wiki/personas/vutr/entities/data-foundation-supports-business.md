The hardest truth I learned is that no matter how fancy your pipeline or infrastructure is, if your data foundation cannot support the business, everything you do is just garbage. Business value is the number one priority — you are hired to operate at the scale the company needs to produce business reports on time, not solely for your ability to debug Spark.

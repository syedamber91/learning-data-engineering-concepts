dbt was created in 2016 by Tristan Handy at RJMetrics. Its adoption exploded from 3 companies in 2016 to 100 in 2017, 5,000+ by 2021, and 9,000+ by 2022. It's now one of the most in-demand data engineering tools: with only dbt, Airflow, and a cloud data warehouse, a company can build a complete data analytics pipeline. A dbt model is purely Jinja + SQL — trackable for changes, roll-back-able, and CI/CD-friendly.
